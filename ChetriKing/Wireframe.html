Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide a comprehensive description of the Chetri King card game, including its rules, variations, strategies, and a brief history. It aims to be the go-to resource for both new players learning the game and experienced players looking to refine their skills.

Audience

Our primary audience includes card game enthusiasts, individuals interested in learning new strategic games, and players of traditional Nepali card games. We aim to attract both casual players looking for fun and competitive players seeking in-depth strategies.
