Game Rules

Learn the basics of how to command your forces and achieve victory in Chetri King.

Core Concepts

Understand the fundamental elements of the game.

The Objective

Your goal is to protect your Kingdom (which has 10 Health Points) while simultaneously attacking and destroying your opponent's Kingdom before the 60-turn limit is reached.

The Battlefield

The game is played on a path consisting of 8 tiles that separate the two opposing Kingdoms. Your units will march across these tiles to reach the enemy.

The Cards

You command units like Archers and Knights by playing their cards. Each card has unique Health (HP) and Attack Power, determining their strength in battle.

Winning the Game

You win by reducing the enemy Kingdom's HP to 0. If 60 turns pass, the player with the most Kingdom HP wins. If HP is tied, the game is a draw.

Turns & Actions

Each turn, you draw one card and may play one card by placing it on the first tile in a lane. You can also choose to save your card to build powerful combinations.

Card Health

A card's health is tracked by its physical rotation. An 8 HP Golem will turn 8 times before being defeated. An Archer with 2 HP will turn twice and then be removed from play.

Gameplay Flow

A typical turn in Chetri King follows a simple sequence.

  1. 1. Draw Phase

    At the start of your turn, you draw one card from your deck, adding it to your hand.

  2. 2. Action Phase

    You may take one action: either play a card from your hand onto an open starting tile, or choose to do nothing and save your cards for a later turn.

  3. 3. Movement Phase

    All of your units already on the battlefield advance one tile forward toward the enemy Kingdom.

  4. 4. Combat Phase

    If one of your units moves onto a tile occupied by an enemy unit, they fight. If your unit reaches the final tile and is unblocked, it attacks the enemy Kingdom, reducing its HP.
