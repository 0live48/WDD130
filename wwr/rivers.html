Explore Our Rivers

Dry Oar Boating offers thrilling rafting adventures on some of the most beautiful rivers in Colorado. Whether you're a beginner or an expert, there's a trip for you.

Colorado River

The Colorado River is perfect for families and beginners. Enjoy calm waters combined with breathtaking views of Glenwood Canyon.

Roaring Fork River

For more excitement, try the Roaring Fork River with moderate rapids that challenge and thrill rafters of all skill levels.

Shoshone Rapids

Experienced rafters can tackle the Shoshone Rapids, known for their fast flow and exhilarating drops.
