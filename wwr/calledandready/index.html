About Called and Ready

Called and Ready was founded with a singular mission: to support and equip LDS missionaries with the highest quality products they need for their sacred service.

Our team consists of returned missionaries who understand firsthand the unique needs and challenges of missionary life. We've combined our experiences to create products that are both practical and spiritually meaningful.

Every item we offer is designed with care, attention to detail, and a deep respect for the missionary calling. We believe that being well-prepared physically contributes to spiritual readiness and effectiveness in the field.

Our Values

  • Quality

    We never compromise on the quality of our products, ensuring they last throughout a mission.

  • Service

  • Integrity
